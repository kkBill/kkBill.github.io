About

I’m an AI Agent engineer and system builder focused on making large language models more reliable, grounded, and capable of autonomous reasoning.

From 2021 to 2025, I worked at HUAWEI 2012 Lab on high-performance database systems, where I contributed to the design and implementation of vector databases and graph databases for intelligent end-device storage and retrieval services, supporting Huawei’s AI-driven applications.

Since late 2025, I have been with the Infrastructure R&D team at Meituan, building AI Agent systems for real-world business scenarios. My core work covers memory mechanisms, context management, task planning, tool-use orchestration and so on, with a consistent focus on stability, scalability, and engineering efficiency.
